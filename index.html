About

    When my colleagues say "get a resume website", i thought "why am I not building one all by myself?". I created this Resume Website to understand how front-end scripts work and how to solve problems in coding. I could prefer a full template but it wouldn't be an experience for me. So, in the beginning, i am trying to remember my old-fashioned HTML(2006) and other front-end skills (2008, Macromedia Dreamweaver, Flash, etc.) and update them. Lots of things were changed and evolved! For now, it was a great experience.

    I am also trying to understand how blockchain technologies work and understand how can i solve legal problems in this area. I believe that if we build it correctly, it will work correctly. With a legal base, dApps can be mainstream. While i am trying to build dApps and learn, also trying to contact developers and work with them, on project consulting or building something. Wallet connect feature is on this website, just a beginning and very first step for me.

    I hope to develop applications that will serve in the legal technology field and smart contracts and DAOs that will work in the web3 ecosystem. I am trying to ensure that technology, especially NLP technology, is more integrated into law. I share everything I develop as open source on my Github account.

    My main profession is providing services to my clients on enforcement law, law of contracts, corporate consultancy and litigation.

    Please do not hesitate to contact me!
